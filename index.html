Name That Truck

Tons of truck fun for truck-loving toddlers! Real trucks! Real work! Real videos!

Created to help vehicle-curious kids learn about all kinds of trucks, NAME THAT TRUCK is a simple game that asks a player to... NAME THAT TRUCK! Once the truck has been correctly identified from a group of similar trucks, the game rewards the player by providing a video of the truck in action and then, more pictures of that type of truck working in the real world!

NAME THAT TRUCK includes audio prompts along with printed words to make the game inclusive and engaging for those children who don't yet know how to read, and to allow older children well on their way to reading to see the truck names written alongside the audio sounds.

Truck horn sound effects, fun animation and continuously-playing gif videos make the game fun and engaging for both the littlest toddlers, kindergarten truck enthusiasts, and their parents, too.

Large, easy-to-understand visual controls make the game simple and intuitive to use without any extra parental help!

NAME THAT TRUCK is 100% toddler approved. The game was invented by 18-month-old Finn Pennekamp, then designed and developed by his mom.

NAME THAT TRUCK is currently under review for inclusion in Apple's App Store.

Screenshots are provided by App Launchpad.
