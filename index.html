Name That Truck

Tons of truck fun for truck-loving toddlers! Real trucks! Real work! Real videos!

NAME THAT TRUCK was created to help vehicle-curious kids learn about all kinds of trucks. It’s a simple game that asks a player to... NAME THAT TRUCK! After correctly identifying a truck from a group of similar trucks, the game rewards the player with a video of the truck in action… and then MORE pictures of that same truck working in the real world!

NAME THAT TRUCK gives audio prompts alongside written prompts to engage both children who don't yet know how to read AND older children well on their way to reading!

Truck horn sound effects, fun animation and repeating gif videos make the game amusing and engaging for the littlest toddlers, kindergarten truck enthusiasts, and their parents, too!

Large, easy-to-understand visual controls make the gameplay simple and intuitive, without requiring any extra parental help!

NAME THAT TRUCK is 100% toddler approved. The game was invented by 18-month-old Finn Pennekamp, then designed and developed by his mom.
